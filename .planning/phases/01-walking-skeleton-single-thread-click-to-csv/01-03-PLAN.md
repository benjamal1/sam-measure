<objective>
Deliver the calibration branch and the join/assembly stage — the two pure modules where the pipeline's
metric truth and its output contract live. `ruler_scale.py` converts a two-click span on a ruler photo
into a pixels/cm factor stored per (date,batch) session (CAL-01, CAL-02), with the macro-ruler 0–0.5 cm
span default per D-10. `build_final_csv.py` merges measurements + calibration on the date/batch key
(CSV-01), converts px→mm (CSV-02), and writes `final.csv` in the exact R-script column names and order
(CSV-03) — the single place that coupling lives (ARCHITECTURE Stage 4). Date rendered from the day
folder per D-09.

Purpose: These two modules meet only at the join, so they are built together as the "the numbers meet
here" slice. Correctness of the column contract (CSV-03) is the one hard compatibility boundary in the
whole project — the R script is not modified. A thread with no matching calibration must fail loudly
even in Phase 1 (the formal hard-fail contract CAL-03/CSV-04 is Phase 2, but Phase 1 must never
fabricate a factor).

Output: `ruler_scale.py`, `build_final_csv.py`, and their test suites. After this plan a user can run
Stage 3 on a ruler photo and Stage 4 on the two intermediate CSVs to get the R-facing `final.csv`
(AC-007, AC-008).
</objective>
